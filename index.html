Tamilanatu Madhuravanam

Tamilanatu Madhuravanam is located in Madurai,Tamilanadu which is renowned for its delicious and unique street food. Some popular local dishes include Jigarthanda (a cold beverage with milk and almond gum), Paruthi Paal (cottonseed milk), and the city is also known for its spicy and flavorful cuisine.

Our Restaurant is famous for its vibrant street food culture. You can find a variety of street snacks and delicacies, including kothu parotta (shredded flatbread mixed with spices and meat), muttai dosa (egg dosa), and kari dosa (meat dosa), among others.

Our Restaurant caters to both vegetarian and non-vegetarian preferences. While the city has a rich array of vegetarian dishes like Pongal, dosa, and sambar, it is also known for its non-vegetarian specialties such as Chettinad chicken curry, mutton kari dosa, and fish curry.

Indian Thali

Indian thali is a wide variety of flavors in one meal.Thali includes small portions of several dishes, such as curries, dal, rice, bread , pickles, chutneys, yogurt, and sometimes desserts. This variety allows diners to experience a range of tastes in a single sitting.
